My product thinking framework
01
Who is the user?
Define the target audience with precision — not a broad segment, but the specific person whose life the product changes.
02
What do they need?
Surface unmet needs through discovery — interviews, observation, feedback loops. Listen more than you speak.
03
How do we address it?
Design solutions that fit the user's context — not the solution that's easiest to build or most exciting to ship.
04
Does this segment create revenue?

05
Does this launch make sense?
